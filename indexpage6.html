Magdalene with the Smoking Flame (also titled in French La Madeleine à la veilleuse, and La Madeleine à la flamme filante) is an oil-on-canvas depiction of Mary Magdalene by French Baroque painter Georges de La Tour, painted in 1640. This version of the painting is currently held and exhibited at the Los Angeles County Museum of Art[1] and the Louvre Museum (La Madeleine a la veilleuse).[2] Other paintings by La Tour containing the Penitent Magdalene are exhibited at the New York Metropolitan Museum of Art,[3] the National Gallery of Art, and the Louvre.
